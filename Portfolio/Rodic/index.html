Rodic Cyclothon Aftermovie

Cinematography

Brief


Rodic Consultants Private Limited is a Delhi based construction consultancy firm. They deliver distinctive and impactful solutions to clients across sectors, through a portfolio of quality services, since it's establishment in the year 2000. The client was hosting a cyclothon, a cycle-marathon in order to spread awareness about global warming and our own health. They wanted to help their employees adopt a better lifestyle and live a healthy life. They wanted to get the event covered, so they contacted me to make an aftermovie.


Mood


On the day of the event, I reached the venue a bit earlier than I was supposed to take a few shots of the preparation. The whole video was shot on Sony A6500 with a Sigma 30mm f1.4 lens. No gimbal or Steadicam was used, the video was shot completely handheld. The client did hook me up with a bike and a rider, I was sitting on the back taking all the moving shots. After taking an ample amount of shots, I edited the video in Final Cut Pro. The graphics were later added in using Adobe After Effects.
