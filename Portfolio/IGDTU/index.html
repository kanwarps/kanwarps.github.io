Brief


Bare Anatomy is India's number 1 brand in customized beauty. They offer a huge range of custom hair and beauty products tailored to the customer's needs. The client wanted me to create a series of short Motion Graphic videos to be used for their social media as marketing collateral.


Mood


After a lot of competitor analysis and working very closely with the client, we came up with a variety of short video ideas which resonated with the client's brand.


Result


By the use of poppy colurs, and visuals, I was succesful to create engaging videos for the client. The client got substantial traction because of these videos through their social media platforms and their business grew substantially.
