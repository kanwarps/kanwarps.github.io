IMS Explainer Video

Motion Graphics

Brief


Headsup Corporation is a Delhi based HR Consultancy firm that focuses primarily on small and mid-sized organizations. Founded in 2017, it's a startup by Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Delhi University graduates. With a small team of 20 employees, Head- sup Corporation has developed a good clientele. Their latest product is an Idea Management System, IMS. The IMS provides a platform for the employees to give ideas or suggestions. By doing so, the employees earn points, turning a simple task of suggesting something into a fun little game. They also have the option of submitting their suggestions or complaints anonymously.The client wanted me to make a motion graphics based explainer video for their upcoming product to attract potential investors and customers.


Mood


After having a brief discussion with their design team, I got to know that they are looking for something with a bit of a hand-drawn effect to go with existing graphics. They provided me with the content and the voiceover. I started working on a storyboard depicting how ideas come to us when we are least expecting them, like while traveling in the train, while using the washroom, etc. But we tend to lose these ideas while we are busy with something else. I made it a point that by the use of this product, we'll be able to save and deliver our ideas and sug- gestions to the right platform. All the graphic elements are designed and animated in Adobe After Effects. The video is originally made in 60 frames per second, but a jittery, conventional hand-drawn animation effect is given by the use of a posterize time effect.
