Altspace App Explainer

Motion Graphics

Brief


AltSpace is a Delhi based startup that provides a platform to not only the people who are looking for a co-working space for their but also to the owners of various co-working spaces. It allows people to list their spaces and find ideal customers. The customers can search for desired spaces in their locality with all the necessary ameni- ties. The client wanted me to make a motion graphic based app explainer video for their upcoming mobile application. The purpose of this video was to showcase how easy it is to book a co-working space using the services AltSpace provides.


Mood


The client didn't want any such voiceover. Rather, they just wanted a simple, music-based video. After discussing with the client, I understood that the best way to showcase the working of their app will be by actually incorporating screenshots from the real app. So I built a storyboard around this idea only.


Result


I used shape layers in Adobe After Effects to create the shape of the phone and the hand. I explained the workflow of the application in this video.
